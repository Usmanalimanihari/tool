How To Use The Image Cropper

Follow these simple steps to crop and optimize your images

1

Upload Your Image

Click the "Choose Image" button or drag and drop your image file into the upload area. We support JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats.

2

Crop Your Image

Use the crop tool to select the area you want to keep. Drag the corners to adjust the crop box or move it around to frame your image perfectly.

3

Adjust Dimensions

Set the exact width and height you need using the sliders. Toggle the "Maintain aspect ratio" option to keep your image proportions.

4

Optimize Quality

Choose the quality level and output format that best suits your needs. Higher quality means larger file sizes.

5

Download Your Image

Click the "Process Image" button and then download your perfectly cropped and optimized image with one click.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image cropper tool

Is my image uploaded to your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats are supported?

You can upload JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WebP images. The output formats available are JPEG, PNG, and WebP. WebP offers excellent compression with good quality.

What's the maximum image size I can process?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (over 10MB) may cause performance issues on some devices. For best results, we recommend images under 5MB.

How does the compression affect image quality?

Higher compression (lower quality values) will result in smaller file sizes but may introduce artifacts. We recommend testing different quality levels (start with 80%) to find the best balance for your needs.

Can I use this tool on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our tool is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability.
